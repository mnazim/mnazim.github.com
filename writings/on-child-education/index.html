On Child Education

(This is an incomplete work, Only purpose of this page is to serve as a place to collect my thoughts.)

You can live without telivision

Unplug your TV set, pick it up, take it out, and smash it and burn it beyond salvage. Period. No excuses. Disadvantages of TV are far greater than the advantages and those advantages can easily be replaced with better alternatives. Want your children to learn from Discovery Channel - everything available on DVD and P2P networks.

Schools are evil

Don't send them off to schools. Schools will damage your child in the ways you cannot imagine. Just like TV, the disadvantages of the schools system far outweigh the advantages. You want your children to learn social skills, encourage them to go to parks and play with other kids in your residential locality.

Reading and writing are primary skills

Teach you children to read and write a couple of languages.(your native language, English, and may be one more language of your liking). The key is to start early; you will be amazed to see how fast your 2 year old kids can pick-up reading, even before they start speaking properly.

Books are awesome

Now give them books. Lot's of them. Not computers, not iPads. The actual physical, paper books. Read with them; write with them but don't force them. Start slow, not more than 30 minutes, once or twice a week. Build up their curiosity a few pages at a time.
